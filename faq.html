Frequently Asked Questions

What is cancer and how does it form? +
Cancer is a disease where cells grow uncontrollably. It can start almost anywhere in the body and may spread to other parts.
How can I book an appointment with a specialist? +
You can book an appointment from your dashboard under the 'Appointments' section. Select your doctor and preferred date/time.
Are my reports and data secure? +
Yes, all patient data is encrypted and securely stored. Only authorized professionals can access it.
What should I do in case of emergency? +
Use the Emergency Help button on your dashboard. You can call an ambulance, notify police, or share your location with a relative.
Can I view my medicine schedule and dosage? +
Yes, the 'My Medicine' tab shows your full prescription, timings, and dosage instructions.
